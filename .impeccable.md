## Design Context

### Users
Content creators who need a fast, reliable, and beautiful way to transcode high-resolution videos for platforms like YouTube and Instagram.

### Brand Personality
Minimal, professional, and effortless. Key attributes: "Minimal", "Techy".

### Aesthetic Direction
A modern, premium **dark mode** interface. The colors should be sophisticated and non-distracting, emphasizing the content.

### Design Principles
1. **Minimalism**: Remove any unnecessary visual noise. Focus on the core tasks of uploading and monitoring.
2. **Premium Dark Theme**: Use sophisticated dark tones (e.g. OKLCH `0.15` for background, not pure black), tinted neutrals, and high-quality accent colors (indigo/cyan).
3. **Smooth Motion**: Use Framer Motion for elegant transitions and feedback.
4. **Creator-Centric UX**: Ensure clarity in video resolution options and processing status.
